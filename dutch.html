    About the Circle

    I started this class in 2024 after wanting to introduce a Dutch program to the university for several years. There is no official Dutch language at program at UChicago despite nearly 120 years of calls to launch one. Our weekly language circle is designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to those looking to hone their Dutch skills. We create a welcoming learning environment to learn at your own pace with tailored instruction.
